Accessibility Statement

Hira Science Academy is committed to making our website and educational content accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

Our Commitments

We strive to follow best practices and standards defined by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). These guidelines explain how to make digital content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

Key Accessibility Features

  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop devices
  • Text content with readable fonts and good color contrast
  • Alt text for important images
  • Keyboard navigation support for menus and links

Limitations

While we are continuously working to improve accessibility, some older files (such as scanned PDFs) may not be fully optimized. We encourage users to contact us if they encounter any barriers.

Contact Us

If you experience any difficulty accessing our content or have suggestions for improvement, please email us at tayyabgchs@gmail.com. We will do our best to resolve the issue promptly.
