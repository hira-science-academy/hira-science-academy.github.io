What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit websites. They help us understand user behavior, improve site functionality, and deliver a better user experience.

How We Use Cookies

We may allow trusted third-party services (like Google) to use cookies for analytics or social sharing features.

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact your experience on our site.
