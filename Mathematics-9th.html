About These Notes

These comprehensive 9th Class Math notes cover the entire Punjab Textbook Board (PCTB) syllabus for 2025. Each chapter includes:

  • Solved exercises with step-by-step solutions
  • Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) for practice
  • Important definitions and formulas
  • Chapter review exercises
  • Clear explanations of key concepts

Prepared by Muhammad Tayyab (MSc Mathematics), these notes are designed to help students master the 9th grade math curriculum and excel in their board examinations.
