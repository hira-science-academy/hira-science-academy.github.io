About Us

Hira Science Academy is a dedicated platform for providing high-quality science and mathematics education for students of classes 9 and 10. Our mission is to make learning accessible, organized, and effective through easy-to-download notes, MCQs, solved numerical problems, and definitions. One of our main goals is to help students achieve full marks in BISE board exams through clear explanations and exam-focused preparation.

We believe that every student deserves the right guidance, and our goal is to support both students and teachers with well-structured, syllabus-based resources that are 100% aligned with board requirements.
